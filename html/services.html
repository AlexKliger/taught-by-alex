Private Tutoring Service

A private tutoring session consists of an hour of one-on-one instruction.

If at the end of the scheduled hour more material remains to be covered, then the session can be extended by an additional 15 minutes without any extra charge.

Service Rate
Lower-level Tutoring Refers to elementary and middle-school level courses.
$60 per session

$80 per session

$100 per session

Distance Learning

A common concern I encounter is whether or not the quality of a tutoring session suffers when given on the internet.

I strive to smooth the transition from traditional tutoring by using streaming quality hardware and learning tools.

With an artist-grade drawing tablet I am able to neatly take notes on my screen that can be seen by the student and saved for later reference.

How to Make a Payment

1. Payment can be made through various platforms such as, but not limited to, PayPal, CashApp, or Venmo.

2. Prior to sending a payment, please schedule at least one session and wait for written confirmation of your appointment.

3. Payment is expected after each session and before any following sessions.

Scheduling

To set up a session or a free 15 minute consultation, feel free to contact me by email or by phone during normal operating hours.

If sending an email, please include the subject of tutoring in the subject line. In the body of your message, indicate:

1. your preferred appointment time, and

2. at least one alternative time.

I look forward to hearing from you!
